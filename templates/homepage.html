Short Story About Our System.

The skin disease detection system uses deep learning algorithms, specifically convolutional neural networks (CNNs), to analyze images of skin diseases and provide potential diagnoses. It is designed for individuals seeking self-assessment of skin conditions and offers valuable support for healthcare professionals. Built primarily in Python, the system aims for high accuracy levels and a more comprehensive database of skin diseases and conditions in the future.
